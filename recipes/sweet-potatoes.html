Chicken Fajita Sweet Potatoes

Description

This recipe will make 6 delicious Chicken Fajita Sweet Potatoes that can easily provide lunch for the workweek. This is an extremely simple recipe and should only take about 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 3 Sweet Potatoes
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 9oz Chicken Breast
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1/4 cup Black Beans
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 cup Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Bake sweet potato until cooked all the way through. Cut potato in half, remove filling and set aside.
  2. Dice and saut√© your red onion, peppers, and chicken breast in a pan until cooked through
  3. Combine Well.
  4. Season your chicken and vegetables with cumin, paprika and some salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add your sweet potato filling to your saute mixture and combine.
  6. Put mixture into potato skins, cover with cheese.
  7. Bake in oven until golden brown.
  8. Let Cool and Enjoy!
