PB Protein Cookies

Description

This recipe will make 10 delicious PB Protein Cookies that can be used as a healthy treat throughout the week. This is a veyr quick recipe and should only take about 10-15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup PB2
  • 1/4 cup Brown Suagr
  • 9oz Chicken Breast
  • 1 Egg & 1 Egg White
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 cup Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Line a tray with parchment paper and scoop cookie batter on to tray.
  3. Bake @350 for 5-8 minutes depending on oven...let cool for 3 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
